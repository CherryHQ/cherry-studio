## What changed

The Quick Access and Automation groups in Settings are now one Efficiency group. Channels and Scheduled Tasks appear first, followed by Keyboard Shortcuts, Quick Assistant, and Selection Assistant.

## Why this matters to the user

Users will find automation, keyboard shortcuts, and assistant shortcuts together under one Settings section, with both assistant entries at the bottom.

## What the user should do

Nothing — the settings, routes, and saved configuration are unchanged.
