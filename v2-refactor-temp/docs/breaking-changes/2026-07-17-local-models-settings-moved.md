## What changed

Local model downloads no longer appear at the bottom of Environment Dependencies. They now have a separate Local Models entry directly below Default Models in Settings.

## Why this matters to the user

Users managing the local embedding and OCR models can open Settings → Local Models without scrolling through runtime dependency tools.

## What the user should do

Nothing — downloaded model files and their current status are unchanged.
